New Personal Best!

by Coach Johnathan

I’m excited to share that I’ve hit a new personal best—squatting 350kg for 6 reps! This has been a challenging but rewarding journey, and it wouldn’t have been possible without a few key changes to my training program.

Over the past few months, I’ve focused on refining my squat technique by improving my range of motion and working on mobility. I also incorporated accessory exercises to strengthen supporting muscle groups, like my glutes and core, which helped tremendously in achieving better stability.

This new PR isn’t just about the weight—it represents progress, resilience, and the ability to push past plateaus. For anyone working toward their fitness goals, remember that small adjustments and consistency can lead to incredible results. Stay focused and trust the process!
